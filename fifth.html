About Rainbow Visitor centre

A new farm visitor centre has opened near Hawick offering guests an ‘authentic’ experience. A small farm located between Edinburgh and Carlisle has been converted to a visitor attraction that includes a shop selling local produce and plants, small petting zoo, cafe, Play Park and walks. It has been 2 years since it's open and the number of visitors is growing day by day. It first opened with a small shop selling local produc and plants and now it has been converted to a decent size of visitor centre.


Stuart is a local farmer and businessman who, along with others diversified into this business in 1999. Stuart has now continue to carry on most of his farming interests to concentrate on Rainbow Visitor Centre.
He has several years of experience in managing other businesses. With these experience and support from staffs and local community, he believes that it will be good future for the visitor center.

Kersten is a familiar face to our regulars, having previously worked in our Gift Shop as Assistant Manager. Kersten has since moved to the position of Restaurant Manager, bringing with her an extensive history and knowledge of the hospitality catering industry, and now manages both the Restaurant and Gift Shop.
